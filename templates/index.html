What is CyberVantage?

CyberVantage is an innovative educational web platform designed to teach students about cyber safety, phishing attacks, and spam detection through a structured, four-phase learning journey. Our platform combines interactive lessons, AI-powered simulations, and personalized feedback to create an engaging and effective cybersecurity education experience.
